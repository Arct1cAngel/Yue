:gear: General Commands :gear:
=============================
:grey_question: **Help:** Get information on Yue
or YueOS
:arrows_counterclockwise: **Refresh:** Refresh Yue!
:thought_balloon: **Suggest:** Make a suggestion
for Yue!
📜 **Legal:** View Yue's legal information.
🗑️ **Purge:** Purge up to 100 messages (Can't purge messages older than 2 weeks).
🏄‍♀️ **removeYue:** Remove Yue from your server and properly delete your data from her database.

:yellow_heart: YueOS :yellow_heart:
=============================
:yellow_square: **Profile:** Set up or view your
stats!
:shield: **Class:** Choose a class and gain their
respective buffs!
:date: **Daily:** Claim your daily paycheque!
:moneybag: **Richest:** See who the current
richest user is (global)
:knife: **Kill:** Yue will attempt to
kill your enemy.
💒 **Marry:** Marry another user.
Cost $1000
❤️‍🔥 **Divorce:** Divorce your spouse.
Cost $2000
🎲 **Roulette:** Play a game of classic Roulette.
<:spade:1239390979950247966> **Blackjack:** Play a game of 1 deck Blackjack.
<:Schlag:1241861137826447511> **Attack:** Attack your enemies and steal their money.

💼 **Inventory:** Bring up your inventory and trading menu!
💸 **Pay:** Pay another user.
🛒 **Shop:** Bring up the daily shop.
