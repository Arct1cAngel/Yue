Items:
=============================
⚔️**Weapons**: High Physical Damage
with lower Crit Rate.

✨**Magic**: Magic Damage with
different Crit Effects and
higher Crit Rate.

👕**Armor**: Protect against Magic

🛡️**Shields**: Protect against 
Weapons

Crit Effects:
=============================
⚔️**Physical:** x1.5 Damage

🔥**Fire:** Burn your enemy
for 1-3 Damage over Time!
(***Burning***)

🧊**Ice:** Freeze your enemy
in place to stop them from
dealing damage!
(***Frozen***)

💧**Water:** Drench your enemy
to make them deal 25% less
damage!
(***Drenched***)

⚡**Lightning:** Strike your
enemy with Higher Crit
Damage but lower Crit Rate.

🍃**Wind:** Slash your enemy
with Higher Crit Rate but
lower Crit Damage.

Heal: ($250)
=============================
♥️ **Healing:** Use /heal 
outside of battle to restore
your health to full and clear
any status effect!
